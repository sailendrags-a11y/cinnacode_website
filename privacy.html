Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information Cinnacode collects, why, and how it's used. Cinnacode is operated by Sailendra, an individual developer based in India.

1. Information we collect

When you register, we collect your name, email address, and country. If you sign in with GitHub, we receive your GitHub username, public profile name, avatar, and email address as provided by GitHub. We also record usage data necessary to operate the credit system — the number of chat messages, completions, and edits you make, and your remaining credit balance.

2. What we do not collect

We do not collect or store payment card details. All payment processing is handled directly by Razorpay; Cinnacode never receives or stores your card number, CVV, or full payment credentials.

3. How your code is handled

When you use a chat, completion, or edit feature, the relevant code or text you're working with is sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate a response. This content is transmitted for the purpose of generating that single response and is subject to Anthropic's own data handling terms. We do not use your code to train any model, and we do not sell your code or personal data to third parties.

4. Third-party service providers

We rely on the following third parties to operate the Service:

  • Anthropic — processes chat/completion requests to generate AI responses.
  • Razorpay — processes payments for credit purchases.
  • Railway — hosts our backend server and database.
  • GitHub — provides OAuth login if you choose to sign in with GitHub.

Each of these providers has its own privacy practices governing the data they process on our behalf.

5. Data retention

We retain your account information and usage history for as long as your account is active. If you'd like your account and associated data deleted, contact us at the email below and we will process the request within a reasonable time.

6. Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us. We will respond to verified requests within a reasonable timeframe.

7. Cookies and local storage

Our website uses minimal local browser storage to support account registration and does not use third-party advertising or tracking cookies.

8. Children's privacy

The Service is not directed at anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact sailendrags@gmail.com, or visit our Contact page.
